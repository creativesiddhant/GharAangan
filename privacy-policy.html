Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

At Ghar Aangan, we value the trust you place in us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and pre-book our premium A2 Desi Pahadi Ghee.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or pre-book a jar/tin of our ghee, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Identification Details: Your full name.
  • Contact Information: Your 10-digit mobile number.
  • Pre-booking Details: Sizing and quantity preference (e.g., 500ml, 1L, 2L, or 5L).
  • Demographics (Optional): Your state and city of residence, to help us optimize regional delivery routing.
  • Technical System Data: When you access our platform, we may automatically collect visitor information including your IP address, country, region, city, and browser user agent to prevent spam and analyze site traffic.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect from you is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To reserve and secure your pre-ordered batch of Pahadi Ghee.
  • To contact you prior to our launch on August 5, 2026, to confirm your order details, shipping address, and send a secure payment link.
  • To communicate updates regarding your booking via SMS, Phone call, or WhatsApp message.
  • To analyze overall website traffic and optimize delivery operations across India.
  • To maintain security and prevent duplicate or fraudulent reservations.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is processed and stored securely. We store all database entries securely through cloud infrastructure using SSL/TLS encryption. We implement standard security practices to protect your contact details from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Since pre-booking requires no financial transactions, credit card or banking details are never collected or stored on our servers.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third-party marketing companies. Your details will only be accessible to:

  • Our internal team at Ghar Aangan to coordinate reservations and deliveries.
  • Trusted courier partners (e.g., Blue Dart, Delhivery) to facilitate shipping once you confirm and complete your order.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or tracking technologies to enhance user experience and record page views. You can modify your browser settings to decline cookies, though some interactive elements might behave differently.

6. Your Rights and Control

You maintain full control over your details. Since pre-booking holds no financial obligations or binding contracts, you can cancel your reservation or ask us to completely remove your details from our records at any time. Simply contact us at manisha.kailash14@gmail.com with your mobile number to request data deletion.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted immediately on this page with an updated revision date.
