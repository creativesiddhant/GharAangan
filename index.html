Frequently Asked Questions

Our products are sourced directly from high-altitude local farms in Uttarakhand. They are grown in a clean, chemical-free environment and processed using traditional heritage methods, like hand-grinding salts on a stone Sil-Batta or wood-boiling jaggery, preserving their natural nutrients and authentic mountain flavor.

Our A2 Desi Pahadi Ghee is crafted from the nutritious A2 milk of free-grazing Badri cows in the high-altitude Himalayan regions of Uttarakhand. It is prepared using the traditional Bilona churned method, where milk is curdled and slowly hand-churned to yield nutrient-rich butter, which is then melted over a firewood stove. This ensures a granular texture, rich aroma, and high content of natural vitamins and healthy fats.

Pisyu Loon translates to "ground salt" in the local Pahadi language. It is a traditional Himalayan salt ground by hand on a stone mortar (Sil-Batta) with native mountain herbs, seeds (like Bhaang/Hemp seeds), and spices, creating a rich, aromatic, and mineral-dense seasoning.

Our jaggery is completely natural and unrefined. Sugarcane juice is slowly boiled over a wood fire and blended with premium ingredients like saunf (fennel seeds), mixed dry fruits, kaju, badam, raisins, or nutritional seeds. No chemical clarifiers, preservatives, or artificial colors are used.
